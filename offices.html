Offices

The third floor of the Arena Center will be used as office areas, by offering an excellent location in the city center, as well as its high quality construction.

Moreover, in the upcoming years, it is foreseen additional areas which will be used as office located on floors 4, 5 and 6, by offering a total office space of c. 8,000 m2.

A unique opportunity for businesses to have a landmark in downtown Tirana. Entrance to the office is from the first floor with dedicated elevators and stairs.
